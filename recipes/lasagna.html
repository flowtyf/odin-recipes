Lasagna Recipe

Description

the best lasagna is here! layered with a rich meat sauce and a creamy parmesan white sauce, plus the perfect amount of mozzarella cheese! no ricotta cheese needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 bag of flour
  • 1 gallon of milk
  • 1 block of parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put the butter in the bag of flour and shake vigorously
  2. Pour the gallon of milk into the bag of flour
  3. Grate the cheese into the bag of flour
  4. Preheat your oven to 500 Degrees Celsius
  5. Place your lasagna flour bag in the oven
  6. Cook for 5 minutes
  7. Nice eat it
